Delicious Pancakes

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder

Instructions:

  1. Mix all dry ingredients in a bowl.
  2. Add wet ingredients and mix until smooth.
